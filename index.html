Hi, I am Felix

PhD Researcher in Rocketry & Control at TU Munich

I am a PhD student at the Chair of Space Mobility and Propulsion (SMP) under the supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. Chiara Manfletti . My research lies at the intersection of rocketry and control and on robust and fault-tolerant control architectures for rocket propulsion systems, with an emphasis on bringing advanced control methods from theory into real-world flight and test-bench applications.
Feel free to reach out!

What motivates and defines me

Pushing the frontiers in research

Working on the theoretical level to improve robustness and agility of rocket propulsion control.

Design & Demonstration

Starting on a blank sheet of paper, our chair are developing the ASCENT platform to demonstrate our research does not only work on paper and in simulations, but in flight / on real hardware!.

Educating & Guiding

Being responsible for the project management and technical development of the ASCENT project gives me the opportunity to guide a team of great students.

Being Curious

I'd like to explore and learn new things. This is true for methods that allow me to solve problems in smart ways, but also applications. Hence, I am very grateful that I had the chance to join the MECO Team in Leuven in 2025, where I was working in robotics instead of rocketry! The ASCENT project allows me to look into heat transfer and other aspects.
