Hey there, I'm Felix!

PhD Candidate in Rocketry & Control at TU Munich

I am a PhD student at the Chair of Space Mobility and Propulsion (SMP) under the supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. Chiara Manfletti. My research lies at the intersection of rocketry and modern control. My work focuses on dynamic modelling, product design, and real-world experimentation.

What motivates and defines me

Advancing Research

Working on the theoretical level to improve robustness and agility of rocket propulsion control

Design & Demonstration

Starting on a blank sheet of paper, we are developing the ASCENT platform to demonstrate our research does not only work on paper and in simulations, but in flight / on real hardware!.

Educating & Guiding

Being responsible for the project management and technical development of the ASCENT project gives me the opportunity to guide a team of great students.

Being Curious

I'd like to explore and learn new things. This is true for methods that allow me to solve problems in smart ways, but also applications. Hence, I am very grateful that I had the chance to join the MECO Team in Leuven in 2025, where I was working in robotics instead of rocketry! The ASCENT project allows me to look into heat transfer and othe raspects.
